Famous Places

Top Tourist Attractions In Telangana

Charminar

Charminar is the symbol of Hyderabad and one of the most famous monuments in India. Built in 1591, it attracts tourists from around the world.

Golconda Fort

Golconda Fort is known for its royal history, massive architecture and unique sound system. It reflects Telangana’s rich heritage.

Ramappa Temple

Ramappa Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for Kakatiya architecture, beautiful carvings and ancient engineering.

Culture Of Telangana

Traditions, Festivals & Food

Bathukamma Festival

Bathukamma is Telangana’s famous flower festival celebrated with joy, traditional songs and cultural dances.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is world-famous for its rich spices, delicious taste and royal cooking style.

Bonalu Festival

Bonalu is a traditional Telangana festival dedicated to Goddess Mahakali, celebrated with music and dance.
